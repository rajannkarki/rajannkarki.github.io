About

My path into tech started with a simple question: "but how does that actually work?" That question pulled me into computers, then into security — where the fun is in understanding a system well enough to find where it bends and where it breaks.

These days I'm studying CS at UT Arlington and chasing cybersecurity with everything I've got. I built a Proxmox home lab so I'd have a safe place to actually attack and defend real machines, I grind boxes on TryHackMe, and I'm working toward my CompTIA Security+. On the development side, I ship small projects in Python, Django, and React because I learn best by building things people can actually use.

When I'm not in a terminal, you'll find me editing video in DaVinci Resolve, tinkering with my dual-boot Linux setup, or going down a rabbit hole on whatever caught my attention that week. I believe in learning in public — so most of what I make ends up on GitHub.
